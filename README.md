# Middlefork Fire/Rescue

A 100% volunteer fire company located near Bowen, Powell County, Kentucky. We operate two engines, a tanker, a light rescue, and a brush truck. Our primary coverage areas include Slade, Bowen, and the eastern third of Powell County. We are an all-hazards fire department, responding to vehicle crashes, off road incidents, structure fires, and wildland fires, among other things.
